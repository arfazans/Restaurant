Our Most Demanded Recipes

Nargisi Kofta (نرگسی کوفتہ)

Nargisi Kofta is a popular dish made with eggs and meat, often served with a rich and flavorful sauce. The koftas are made by wrapping hard-boiled eggs in a mixture of minced meat and spices, and then deep-frying them. The sauce is made with a blend of spices, onions, and tomatoes, giving it a rich and tangy flavor. Nargisi Kofta is a delicious and satisfying meal that's perfect for special occasions.

Price : $15

Chicken Qorma (چکن قورما)

Chicken Qorma is a rich and creamy curry dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a flavorful sauce. The sauce is made with a blend of spices, yogurt, and nuts, giving it a smooth and velvety texture. The chicken is cooked to perfection, absorbing the flavors of the sauce. It's a delicious and satisfying meal that's perfect for any time of day.

Price : $16

Vegetable Biryani (سبزی بریانی)

Vegetable Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with a mix of vegetables, basmati rice, and spices. The dish is cooked with a blend of whole spices, herbs, and saffron, giving it a rich and exotic flavor. The vegetables are cooked to perfection, retaining their texture and flavor. It's a delicious and satisfying meal that's perfect for any time of day.

Price : $12

Beef Stew

Beef stew is a hearty, comforting dish made by slow-cooking tender chunks of beef with a medley of vegetables like carrots, potatoes, onions, and celery in a rich, savory broth often flavored with herbs. Typically simmered for hours, this classic one-pot meal is enjoyed around the world and is a timeless favorite for cozy family dinners.

Price : $10

Chicken Burger

A chicken burger is a popular and satisfying sandwich that features a seasoned, grilled or fried chicken breast or patty served on a toasted bun, often accompanied by fresh toppings like lettuce, tomato, onions, and pickles. It's a go-to choice for casual dining and fast food alike.

Price : $9
