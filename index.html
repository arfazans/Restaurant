Our Most Demanded Recipes

Calzones (Italian Pizza)

Calzones, a delicious Italian creation often considered a folded pizza, are savory turnovers made from pizza dough that's filled with a variety of ingredients such as mozzarella, ricotta, cured meats, and vegetables, then sealed and baked or fried to golden perfection; originating in Naples, calzones are a portable, mess-free alternative to traditional pizza, offering the same irresistible flavors in a compact form, with regional variations across Italy and worldwide.
Price : $15

Tilapia Meunière

Tilapia Meunière is a classic French-inspired dish where tender tilapia fillets are lightly dredged in flour, pan-fried in butter until golden brown, and finished with a simple yet flavorful sauce made from browned butter, fresh lemon juice, and chopped parsley, creating a perfectly balanced combination of richness and acidity; often served with sides like steamed vegetables, rice, or potatoes, this elegant yet easy-to-prepare meal highlights the delicate texture of the fish while enhancing its mild flavor, making it a favorite for both casual weeknight dinners and refined dining occasions.
Price : $20

Pho Tom

Phở Tôm is a fragrant and flavorful Vietnamese noodle soup featuring succulent shrimp as the star ingredient, served in a steaming, aromatic broth made from simmered seafood stock, infused with traditional spices like star anise, cloves, ginger, and garlic; the dish includes flat rice noodles (bánh phở), fresh herbs such as Thai basil, cilantro, and scallions, along with bean sprouts, lime wedges, and chili slices for customization; Phở Tôm offers a lighter, ocean-inspired twist on the classic beef or chicken phở, delivering a refreshing yet deeply satisfying experience with every spoonful.
Price : $35

Beef Stew

Beef stew is a hearty, comforting dish made by slow-cooking tender chunks of beef with a medley of vegetables like carrots, potatoes, onions, and celery in a rich, savory broth often flavored with herbs such as thyme, bay leaves, and rosemary; typically simmered for hours to allow the flavors to meld and the meat to become melt-in-your-mouth tender, this classic one-pot meal is enjoyed around the world with countless regional variations, from wine-laced French boeuf bourguignon to tomato-rich Italian spezzatino, making it a timeless favorite for cozy family dinners and cold-weather comfort.
Price : $10

Chicken Burger

A chicken burger is a popular and satisfying sandwich that features a seasoned, grilled or fried chicken breast or patty served on a toasted bun, often accompanied by fresh toppings like lettuce, tomato, onions, and pickles, along with condiments such as mayonnaise, mustard, or spicy aioli; this versatile meal can be customized with cheese, bacon, or avocado for extra flavor, and is enjoyed worldwide as a lighter alternative to traditional beef burgers, offering a delicious balance of crisp textures, juicy meat, and bold flavors that make it a go-to choice for casual dining and fast food alike.
Price : $9
