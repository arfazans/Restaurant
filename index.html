Our Most Demanded Recipes

Nargisi Kofta (نرگسی کوفتہ)

Nargisi Kofta is a popular dish made with eggs and meat, often served with a rich and flavorful sauce. The koftas are made by wrapping hard-boiled eggs in a mixture of minced meat and spices, and then deep-frying them. The sauce is made with a blend of spices, onions, and tomatoes, giving it a rich and tangy flavor. Nargisi Kofta is a delicious and satisfying meal that's perfect for special occasions. Its unique flavors and textures make it a standout dish in any South Asian cuisine. It's often served at weddings and other celebrations.

Chicken Qorma (چکن قورما)

Chicken Qorma is a rich and creamy curry dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a flavorful sauce. The sauce is made with a blend of spices, yogurt, and nuts, giving it a smooth and velvety texture. The chicken is cooked to perfection, absorbing the flavors of the sauce. Chicken Qorma is a popular dish in many South Asian countries and is often served on special occasions. It's a delicious and satisfying meal that's perfect for any time of day, and its mild flavors make it a great option for those who prefer less spicy food.
Price : $16

Vegetable Biryani (سبزی بریانی)

Vegetable Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with a mix of vegetables, basmati rice, and spices. The dish is cooked with a blend of whole spices, herbs, and saffron, giving it a rich and exotic flavor. The vegetables are cooked to perfection, retaining their texture and flavor. Vegetable Biryani is a popular dish in many South Asian countries and is often served on special occasions. It's a delicious and satisfying meal that's perfect for any time of day.
Price : $12

Beef Stew

Beef stew is a hearty, comforting dish made by slow-cooking tender chunks of beef with a medley of vegetables like carrots, potatoes, onions, and celery in a rich, savory broth often flavored with herbs such as thyme, bay leaves, and rosemary; typically simmered for hours to allow the flavors to meld and the meat to become melt-in-your-mouth tender, this classic one-pot meal is enjoyed around the world with countless regional variations, from wine-laced French boeuf bourguignon to tomato-rich Italian spezzatino, making it a timeless favorite for cozy family dinners and cold-weather comfort.
Price : $10

Chicken Burger

A chicken burger is a popular and satisfying sandwich that features a seasoned, grilled or fried chicken breast or patty served on a toasted bun, often accompanied by fresh toppings like lettuce, tomato, onions, and pickles, along with condiments such as mayonnaise, mustard, or spicy aioli; this versatile meal can be customized with cheese, bacon, or avocado for extra flavor, and is enjoyed worldwide as a lighter alternative to traditional beef burgers, offering a delicious balance of crisp textures, juicy meat, and bold flavors that make it a go-to choice for casual dining and fast food alike.
Price : $9
